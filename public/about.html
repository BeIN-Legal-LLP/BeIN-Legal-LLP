About the firm

A practice built on precision and trust.

BeIN Legal LLP is a corporate legal services firm with offices in Delhi NCR, Hyderabad, and Indore — advising businesses on the matters that shape how they grow.

Who we are

Counsel for the businesses building India.

We work with founders, in-house counsel, and management teams across sectors — from early-stage startups taking their first steps through incorporation, to mid-market businesses managing multi-state compliance and complex commercial relationships.

Our practice is organised around five areas: corporate & startups, intellectual property, contracts, regulatory compliance, and dispute resolution. Within each, we aim to deliver work that is rigorous in its analysis and practical in its application — strategy that holds up in the boardroom and in court.

Every engagement begins with understanding the commercial context. The legal answer to most questions is not a one-line opinion — it's a judgement about risk, cost, time, and the relationships involved. We do that work for you, then explain it plainly.

How we work

Six commitments we hold ourselves to.

Clarity over jargon

Legal advice that you cannot act on is not advice. We write in plain English, explain trade-offs in commercial terms, and avoid the hedging that obscures more than it protects.

Partner-led work

The lawyer who takes your call is the lawyer who works on your matter. Important decisions are not delegated, and the person briefing you understands the file end-to-end.

Fixed fees where possible

For defined work — incorporations, trademark filings, standard contracts — we quote fixed fees agreed in writing before we start. No hourly surprises.

Speed when speed matters

Some matters can wait a week. Others cannot. We say so honestly, and we hold ourselves to the timelines we agree.

Confidentiality first

Client information is handled with the highest standards of security and discretion — both because the law requires it and because trust is the foundation of the relationship.

Long-term thinking

The cheapest answer today is often the most expensive answer in five years. We give the advice we would want our own businesses to receive — even when it's not the easiest path.

Our practice

What we work on.

We organise our work around five practice areas. Most engagements draw on more than one — that's the point of having them under one roof.

Corporate & startups — incorporation of companies, LLPs, partnerships, and OPCs; founder agreements; cap-table structuring; ROC and MCA compliance.

Intellectual property — trademark prosecution from filing through hearing and renewal; copyright and design registration; patent advisory; IP enforcement and infringement matters.

Contracts — drafting, vetting, and negotiating commercial contracts across procurement, distribution, services, employment, and shareholder arrangements.

Regulatory & compliance — GST, MSME, FSSAI, IEC, Shops & Establishment, ISO certifications, and ongoing statutory compliance for operating businesses.

Dispute resolution — commercial litigation, arbitration, and pre-litigation negotiation, with a focus on early settlement where it serves the client's interest.

Have a matter to discuss?

An initial conversation is free and confidential — usually a 30-minute call to understand your situation and what good legal support would look like.
