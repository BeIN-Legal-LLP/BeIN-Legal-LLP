Join us

Build your practice with us.

We're a small, focused team that takes the work seriously and the egos lightly. If that sounds like a good fit, we'd like to hear from you.

What we look for

Lawyers, not technicians.

We hire for judgement, curiosity, and the discipline to do work that holds up under scrutiny — qualifications matter, but they're the floor, not the ceiling.

We work on a wide mix of matters across corporate, IP, contracts, compliance, and disputes. That breadth means there's always something new to learn and no two weeks look the same. The trade-off is that we expect people to think carefully, ask questions, and own their work — including the parts that are uncomfortable to surface.

Whether you're an LLB graduate looking for your first proper opportunity, a company secretary or compliance professional thinking about a move, or an experienced practitioner who wants to build a focused corporate practice with us — get in touch.

How we work together

A few things that matter to us.

Real responsibility, early

You'll work directly on client matters from the start — no years of carrying briefcases. Mistakes happen; we use them to teach, not to punish.

Clear feedback

You should always know how you're doing and what would make you better. We give feedback often, plainly, and in both directions — we expect to receive it too.

Respect for your time

Long hours happen — particularly around filings and hearings — but we don't perform busyness. If the work is done, the day is done.

Investment in your growth

We support continuing education, professional memberships, and time spent on conferences and bar work. Growing the team's expertise is part of the firm's job.

Multi-office flexibility

We operate across Delhi NCR, Hyderabad, and Indore. Where you sit is a function of where the work is and what suits your life — we're flexible.

Honest about trade-offs

We're not the highest-paying firm in the market, and we won't pretend to be. What we offer is interesting work, genuine ownership, and a place where the small team makes the difference.
